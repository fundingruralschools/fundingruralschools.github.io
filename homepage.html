How much did your school district get?

As California natives, we understand what it can be like to go to school in largely rural communities, etc etc.

While students at Stanford University, Lee ('25) and Charlie ('27) created this web app as part of the course "Building News Applications" with Serdar Turmgoren. Using data provided by the National Forest Service, this interactive map allows the user to see how much funding their school district got from the Secure Rural Schools Act, which began in 1908 and failed to be renewed by congress for the first time in 2024.
